Jennifer Strang
Charleston, SC

Jennifer Strang has spent her life effortlessly helping people just trying to find their way in the United States. Jennifer can answer your questions and provide you with a clear explanation of immigration law as it pertains to your unique situation because our practice offers personalized attention to every client.
